Subscription FAQs

Can I switch products after subscribing?

Yes — you can switch between capsules, powder, or the bundle from your account dashboard at any time before your next billing date.

What if I want to pause instead of cancel?

You can skip any shipment or pause your subscription for up to 3 months. Your subscription will resume automatically after the pause period unless you cancel.

When will I be charged and when does it ship?

You're charged on the same day each month (or quarter) from the date of your first order. Shipments go out within 1–2 business days of billing and typically arrive within 3–5 business days.
